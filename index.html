Mission

The mission of Engineering Student Council is to (1) organize social, academic, and service activities for the SEAS community, (2) provide resources to support engineering student organizations and programs, (3) function as a liaison between students faculty and administration, and (4) identify and address opportunities for improvement within the engineering community.

Govern

We are the governing body for the engineering school.

Collaborations

The University's Engineering Student Council collaborates with a variety of organizations, including over 40 Engineering CIOs, each of the departments in the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, and a variety of non-profit organizations in the community. In addition, our board is comprised of two representatives from the Student Council, Honor Committee, and University Judiciary Committee.
